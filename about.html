About Us

Christ Gospel Apostolic Church, The Perfection Chapel, is a holiness-driven, spirit-filled apostolic church committed to preaching the full gospel of Christ. We believe in the total transformation of lives through the power of the Word, worship, and the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of spiritually mature believers who live in complete obedience to God’s Word and are rapture-ready.

Our Mission

To preach Christ, teach holiness, heal the brokenhearted, and prepare the saints for the coming of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.
